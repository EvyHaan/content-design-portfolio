WORK HELLO

Handshake Mobile App

(Concept Piece)


The Problem


The team at Handshake identified a need for a common ‘hub’ where freelancers and project owners can share in project management. While there are apps available in this space, most fall short in one of these ways:


  1. Focus on matching freelancers and project owners
  2. Provide management services for just one group
  3. Have a relatively impersonal or clunky interface

The team would like to create an intuitive and engaging tool that enhances productivity while supporting the partnership.



What I Did


  • Competitive Analysis
  • Content Design
  • Microcopy


The Solution


The voice and tone of the app were crafted to be engaging but ambient. It is approachable without being distracting. It supports the user’s autonomy by being pointed and intuitive.


Thinking in the long-term for feature expansion, maintainability, scalability, and regional market support helped in developing a foundational content strategy and style guide.



The Big 'So What?'


There’s an app for everything. Multiple apps, actually. A good app will go beyond ‘it’s a tool that does the job’. It anticipates, preempts, and becomes an augment rather than a tool. Improving the human experience in the project management space creates a better user experience.



Highlights


Welcome Screen

The welcome screen was a fairly blank slate. The important thing to convey was the benefits Handshake offers. Another addition was the name of the app for brand recognition.

Sign In / Sign Up Screen

The initial mock-ups included a single screen for both flows. I proposed separating them, to reduce user cognitive load.

Dashboard - invoice screen (project owner view)

The initial mock-ups lacked clarity. My goal here was to provide scannable structure to the information.



New screen: User dashboard

One screen that was missing from the mock-ups was a space where a user could manage all their projects. My proposed design includes a filterable list of projects with highlights. Additionally, this dashboard provides a few quick-action and menu elements for easy management.
