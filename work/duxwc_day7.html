Daily UX Writing Challenge: Day 7

Notification

(concept piece)

The Problem

The Scenario

A sports fan is at a wedding while their favorite team is playing against their arch-rivals. Their team scores.

The Challenge

How would you, quickly, let the sports fan know about the latest play, the current score, and the key players? Write it.

Headline: 30 characters max
Body: 45 characters max

What I Did

  • UX copy design

The Solution

A push notification is well-suited to this scenario of discretely communicating brief updates.

This is also an appropriate space to bend the ‘no-jargon’ best practice. Fans of the sport are likely familiar enough with specialized or slang terminology. These terms may act as a shortcut to describe the gist of the play.

The Big 'So What?'

Best practices should not be applied as hard and fast rules to every situation. Generally, it’s a good idea to follow them: there is a reason they are ‘best practices’. However, they may be flexible to the audience, the topic, and the app’s personality.
